<p><strong>The Access Control Fingerprint Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Access Control Fingerprint Chip market is primarily categorized into two types: capacitive and optical fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules use electrical signals to capture print patterns from the finger's surface, providing high accuracy and durability. In contrast, optical fingerprint modules utilize light to create an image of the fingerprint, often offering greater ease of integration in various devices. Both technologies cater to diverse security needs, enhancing biometric authentication in access control systems across multiple industries.</p></p>
