<p><ul><li>N-type Layer</li><li>P-type Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GaAs LPE (Liquid Phase Epitaxy) epitaxial wafer market is categorized primarily into N-type and P-type layers. N-type layers are doped with elements like silicon or tellurium, enhancing electron mobility and conductivity, making them ideal for high-speed electronic and optoelectronic devices. In contrast, P-type layers, doped with elements like zinc or magnesium, facilitate the creation of holes for positive charge carriers, essential for applications in lasers and photodetectors. Both types are critical for advanced semiconductor technologies.</p></p>
