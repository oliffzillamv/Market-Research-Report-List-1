<p><p>The plastic ready meal tray market is categorized into three main types: PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PP (Polypropylene), and Others. PET trays are favored for their lightweight, clarity, and recyclability, making them popular for packaging fresh and refrigerated meals. PP trays offer greater heat resistance and durability, suitable for microwave use. The 'Others' category encompasses a variety of alternative materials, such as biodegradable plastics and multi-layer composites, catering to niche markets focused on sustainability and specific food storage requirements.</p></p>
