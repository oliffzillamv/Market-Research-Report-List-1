<p><p>Thermal management materials for microelectronics encompass various types designed to effectively dissipate heat. Conductive pastes are used for bonding components while ensuring thermal conductivity. Conductive tapes provide easy application and robust thermal connections. Phase change materials adapt their state to enhance heat transfer efficiency. Gap fillers are employed to eliminate air gaps between surfaces, improving thermal contact. Thermal greases create a thermally conductive interface, ensuring optimal heat transfer between components and heat sinks, crucial for device performance and longevity.</p></p>
