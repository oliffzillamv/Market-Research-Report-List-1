<p><strong>The Airway Clearance Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP)</li><li>Oscillatory Positive Expiratory Pressure (OPEP)</li><li>High Frequency ChestWall Compression</li><li>Intrapulmonary Percussive Ventilation</li><li>Mechanical Cough Assist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airway Clearance Systems market comprises several types designed to enhance pulmonary function. Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) aids in maintaining airway opening during exhalation, while Oscillatory Positive Expiratory Pressure (OPEP) combines PEP with vibrations to further mobilize mucus. High-Frequency Chest Wall Compression utilizes rapid inflatable vests to facilitate mucus clearance. Intrapulmonary Percussive Ventilation employs positive pressure pulses for airway clearance, and Mechanical Cough Assist simulates natural coughing to expel secretions effectively, improving respiratory health in patients.</p></p>
