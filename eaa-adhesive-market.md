<p><ul><li>Film Grade</li><li>Extrusion Coating Grade</li><li>Dispersible Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EAA adhesive market features three primary types: Film Grade, Extrusion Coating Grade, and Dispersible Grade. Film Grade is designed for applications that require flexibility and durability in thin layers, commonly used in packaging films. Extrusion Coating Grade is tailored for bonding layers in various materials during the extrusion process, enhancing performance in food packaging and insulation. Dispersible Grade facilitates easy incorporation into aqueous systems, making it suitable for adhesives and coatings that require uniform distribution and application efficiency.</p></p>
