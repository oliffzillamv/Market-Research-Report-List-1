<p><p>Tablet coaters are essential in the pharmaceutical industry for enhancing tablet properties. The standard coating pan provides even distribution of the coating material but can be less efficient for large batches. Perforated coating pans improve airflow, enabling faster and more uniform coating for greater efficiency in industrial settings. Fluidized bed coaters utilize a stream of air to suspend tablets, allowing for uniform coating with a higher capacity and reduced processing times, making them popular for large-scale production environments.</p></p>
