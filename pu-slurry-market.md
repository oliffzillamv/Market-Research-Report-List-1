<p><ul><li>Dry Method</li><li>Wet Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PU slurry market is divided into two main types: dry method and wet method. In the dry method, polyurethane is processed in a solid form, minimizing moisture and enhancing stability, making it suitable for applications requiring controlled conditions. Conversely, the wet method involves mixing polyurethane with liquids, creating a slurry that facilitates easier application and better integration into various materials. Each method caters to different industrial needs, impacting production processes and final product characteristics.</p></p>
