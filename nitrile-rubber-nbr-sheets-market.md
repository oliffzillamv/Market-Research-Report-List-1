<p><p>The nitrile rubber (NBR) sheets market is categorized into three main types: HNBR sheets, XNBR sheets, and others. HNBR (hydrogenated nitrile butadiene rubber) sheets offer enhanced heat and chemical resistance, making them suitable for harsh environments. XNBR (carboxylated nitrile butadiene rubber) sheets provide improved mechanical properties and oil resistance, ideal for automotive applications. The "Others" category encompasses various specialized formulations, meeting unique industry demands in sectors like food processing, pharmaceuticals, and construction, ensuring versatility and performance.</p></p>
