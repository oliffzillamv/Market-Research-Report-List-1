<p><p>Gas Chromatography (GC) is a highly analytical technique used to separate and analyze compounds that can be vaporized without decomposition. It is widely employed in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, food and beverage, and petrochemicals. The method relies on a sample being injected into a chromatographic column, where it is carried by an inert gas. Different components of the sample interact with the stationary phase of the column, separating them based on their volatilities and affinities.</p><p>The Gas Chromatography (GC) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period.
