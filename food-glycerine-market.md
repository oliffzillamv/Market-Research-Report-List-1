<p><p>The food glycerine market is divided into two main types: food grade and feed grade. Food grade glycerine is highly purified and safe for human consumption, often used as a sweetener, humectant, and preservative in various food products, as well as in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. Feed grade glycerine, on the other hand, is suitable for animal feed applications, providing an energy source and moisture retention. Both grades play vital roles in their respective industries, ensuring quality and safety for consumers and livestock.</p></p>
