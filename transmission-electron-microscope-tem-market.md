<p><p>The Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM) market includes several types, each serving specific imaging and analysis needs. Conventional TEMs provide high-resolution imaging by transmitting electrons through thin samples. Low-Voltage Electron Microscopes operate at reduced voltages, minimizing damage to sensitive samples while maintaining good resolution. Cryo-microscopy involves cooling samples to preserve their native state, enabling the study of biological specimens without alteration. Together, these TEM types cater to diverse applications in materials science, biology, and nanotechnology research.</p></p>
