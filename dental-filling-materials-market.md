<p><p>The dental filling materials market consists of various types, including silver amalgam, composite resin, glass ionomer cements, and others. Silver amalgam is a durable, cost-effective choice often used in posterior teeth for its strength. Composite resin offers aesthetic benefits and is suitable for both anterior and posterior fillings due to its tooth-like appearance. Glass ionomer cements provide fluoride release, making them beneficial for patients at risk of decay. Other materials may include resin ionomers and ceramic fillings, each catering to specific dental needs.</p></p>
