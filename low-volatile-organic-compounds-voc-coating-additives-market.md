Wetting agents promote better adhesion and spreadability on surfaces, while dispersants stabilize pigment distribution, preventing sedimentation. Other additives may include biocides and anti-settling agents, all contributing to improved performance, sustainability, and functionality of low-VOC coatings in various applications.</p></p>
