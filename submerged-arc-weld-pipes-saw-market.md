<p><p>Submerged Arc Weld (SAW) pipes are vital in various industries, primarily for transporting fluids and gases. The market comprises two main types: SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welded) pipes, characterized by their spiral-welded seams that enhance strength and flexibility, suitable for large diameters and high-stress applications; and LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welded) pipes, featuring longitudinal seams ideal for thicker wall pipes, used extensively in oil and gas sectors. Both types are critical for infrastructure projects and energy distribution.</p></p>
