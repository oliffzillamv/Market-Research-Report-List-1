<p><p>The cervical cancer market encompasses various diagnostic and screening methods. Pap testing, or Pap smears, involves collecting cells from the cervix to detect abnormalities. HPV testing identifies the presence of human papillomavirus, a key risk factor for cervical cancer. Colposcopy is a follow-up procedure that uses a magnifying instrument to closely examine the cervix. Cervical biopsies involve taking tissue samples for more definitive diagnosis. Each method plays a crucial role in early detection and management of cervical cancer.</p></p>
