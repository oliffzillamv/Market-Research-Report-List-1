<p><p>The IGBT Static Synchronous Compensator market is divided into two main types: Low Voltage STATCOM and High Voltage STATCOM. Low Voltage STATCOMs are designed for applications requiring voltage regulation and reactive power compensation at lower voltage levels, typically in distribution networks. In contrast, High Voltage STATCOMs cater to transmission systems, enhancing grid stability and efficiency at higher voltage levels. Both types improve power quality, mitigate voltage fluctuations, and support renewable energy integration, playing crucial roles in modern electrical systems.</p></p>
