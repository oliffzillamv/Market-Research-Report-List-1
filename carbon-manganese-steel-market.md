<p><p>The carbon manganese steel market is divided into two main types: High Mn Type and Ordinary Type. High Mn Type steel, characterized by a higher manganese content, offers enhanced strength and ductility, making it suitable for applications like automotive parts and structures under impact stress. In contrast, Ordinary Type carbon manganese steel, featuring lower manganese levels, is more cost-effective and widely used in general construction and manufacturing where specific mechanical properties are less critical. Both types serve distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
