<p><strong>The Thermally Modified Wood Boards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermo-S</li><li>Thermo-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermally modified wood boards are categorized into two main types: Thermo-S and Thermo-D. Thermo-S refers to a process that focuses on improving dimensional stability and resistance to biological threats, making it suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, Thermo-D emphasizes enhanced mechanical properties and surface aesthetics, ideal for interior uses. Both types undergo heat treatment, which alters the wood's cellular structure, resulting in increased durability and reduced moisture absorption, thus expanding their application potential in various environments.</p></p>
