<p><strong>The Interior Wall Decoration Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Waterborne"</li><li>"Solvent-Borne"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interior wall decoration coating market is predominantly categorized into two types: waterborne and solvent-borne coatings. Waterborne coatings utilize water as a primary solvent, offering low levels of volatile organic compounds (VOCs), making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. They provide excellent adhesion and durability. In contrast, solvent-borne coatings use organic solvents, resulting in a more robust finish that is resistant to stains and wear, but they may emit higher VOCs. Each type caters to specific aesthetic and functional preferences in interior design.</p></p>
