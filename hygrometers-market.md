<p><strong>The Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive</li><li>Resistive</li><li>Thermal</li><li>Gravimetric</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hygrometers market is categorized into several types based on their measurement principles. Capacitive hygrometers detect humidity using changes in capacitance. Resistive hygrometers rely on changes in electrical resistance of materials in varying humidity levels. Thermal hygrometers measure humidity based on temperature changes in a given environment. Gravimetric hygrometers determine moisture content by weighing a substance before and after drying. Additionally, there are other specialized types that cater to unique applications, enhancing the versatility of humidity measurement.</p></p>
