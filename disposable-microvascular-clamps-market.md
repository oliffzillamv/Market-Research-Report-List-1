<p><strong>The Disposable Microvascular Clamps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The Disposable Microvascular Clamps market is categorized into two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable clamps. Absorbable clamps are designed to gradually dissolve in the body after fulfilling their purpose, minimizing the need for additional surgical removal and reducing long-term complications. Non-absorbable clamps, on the other hand, are made from durable materials and remain in the body permanently, providing consistent mechanical support. Both types are crucial in microvascular surgeries for achieving precise vessel occlusion and facilitating surgical procedures.</p></p>
